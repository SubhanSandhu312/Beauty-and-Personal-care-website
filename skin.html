Best Skincare Techniques

  • Double Cleansing: Start with an oil-based cleanser, followed by a water-based cleanser.
  • Exfoliation: Use a gentle exfoliator twice a week to remove dead skin cells.
  • Layering Products: Apply skincare in the correct order: cleanser, toner, serum, moisturizer, and sunscreen.
  • Hydration: Use hydrating masks and serums to boost your skin's moisture levels.
  • SPF Protection: Always use sunscreen, even on cloudy days.
